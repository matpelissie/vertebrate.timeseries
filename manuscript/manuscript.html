Methods

The database considered contains 830 species of terrestrial vertebrates and 3402` population time series distributed worldwide. We only focused on time series beginning after 1980 in order to be consistent with the climate data we analyse in the following part.

Each population time series was fitted with a linear regression model to quantify the trend through:

\[\begin{eqnarray*} X(t) = \alpha_{0} + \alpha_{1}t + \epsilon(t) \end{eqnarray*}\]
